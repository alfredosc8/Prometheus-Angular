Timer and Summary metrics can be split into predefined buckets.
Each Bucket counts, how many of the metric measurements do fit in the bucket (meaning they have the same, or a bigger value).
If you are not sure which buckets you will need, you can choose the option to publishPercentileHistogram (on metrics creation) than you will get predefined buckets.
